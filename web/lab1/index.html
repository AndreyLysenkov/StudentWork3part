Numbers

The most basic type of number is an integer, a series of digits wich can start with a plus or minus sign.

1 ,23 ,and -100000 are examples.

Commas are not allowed in numbers, but underscores are. So if you feel the need to mark your thousands so the numbers are more readable, use an underscore.

population = 12_000_000_000

Decimal numbers are called floats in Ruby. Floats consisst of numbers with a decimal place or scientific notation.

3.14 ,-808.08 and 12.043e-04 are examples.
